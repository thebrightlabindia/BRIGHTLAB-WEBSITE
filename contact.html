For Prospective Students

If you are interested in joining BRIGHT Lab as a Ph.D. student, M.Tech/M.Sc. student, or research intern, please send your CV and a brief statement of interest to Dr. Pandey. We are looking for motivated individuals with backgrounds in:

  • Optics and Photonics
  • Biomedical Engineering
  • Physics or Chemistry
  • Computer Science / Machine Learning
  • Biotechnology or Biosciences

Application Requirements:

  • Updated CV/Resume
  • Academic transcripts
  • Statement of purpose (1-2 pages)
  • Two letters of recommendation (for Ph.D. applicants)
  • Research publications (if available)

Research Collaborations

We welcome collaborations with academic institutions, healthcare organizations, and industry partners interested in biophotonics research and biomedical technology development. Please contact Dr. Pandey to discuss potential collaboration opportunities.

Areas of Collaboration

  • Clinical validation of diagnostic technologies
  • Development of novel biophotonic instruments
  • AI/ML algorithm development for biomedical applications
  • Technology transfer and commercialization
  • Joint research projects and grant applications
