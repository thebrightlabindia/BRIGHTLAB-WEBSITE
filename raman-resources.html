Raman Resources

"Researcher's life made easy — powerful spectral analysis tools, free for the world."

BRIGHT Lab is committed to making cutting-edge spectral analysis accessible to researchers everywhere. Our free web tools let you upload your Raman data and apply advanced statistical and machine learning methods — no coding required.

Featured Tool

📊
Dimensionality Reduction

PCA • LDA • QDA — Analytical Chemometrics GUI

By Aryan Jaggi

Principal Component Analysis, Linear Discriminant Analysis, Quadratic Discriminant Analysis

Upload your raw Raman spectral data and instantly visualize sample groupings through interactive plots. Identify hidden patterns, reduce dimensionality, and explore variance across your dataset — all in your browser, no installation needed.

📁 CSV / Excel upload 📈 Interactive scores plot 📉 Loadings plot 🎯 Variance explained 💾 Export results
